About the School


Established in 1965 in Uttar Pradesh, Janta Inter College Kotwa Kala - Kushi Nagar, is the only school within a 10-kilometer radius. This school serves the selfless act of free education in underserved area for children. We are focusing more on empowering girls through education which is a nation mission as well. We believe that every child, regardless of their background deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to build a brighter future.
